About Us

Welcome to our world we are a group of eclectic musicians from all different walks of life sharing our love of percussion instruments around New York City. We play the Bass & Snare Drums, Xylophone, Timpani plus the bongos and tambourine. You can often find us playing free shows at verious city parks!

Tour Info

We are currently working with different city agencies and groups to perform more free shows and share our love of music at different MTA subway locations as well as some other non-profit organizations! Once we finalize some dates they will be posted here and sent out via our newletter along with new song release and performance videos,so be sure to sign-up!
